make it make sense

2024 | fucked up brain

this makes no sense
this feeling in my chest

everything's okay
so why is this still playing in my brain

i'm so fed up
with this nonsense

i don't know if i can take it
much longer

pencil

2024 | fucked up brain

When learning to write
I held the pencil wrong

This caused my hand to cramp
since I held the pencil wrong

But I kept writing, to learn how to write
even if I was holding the pencil wrong

And now, after all those years
of holding the pencil wrong

I have a crooked finger
in my dominant hand

I'm not exaggerating
the literal bone structure changed

A permanent reminder
that all those years
no adult noticed
I was holding the pencil wrong
